AlphaBuild 0.1

Very first stable build of the game.
Unfortunately, since tower targeting and other aspects are not yet implemented, all you can do is lose! Sorry!
Items implemented:

  • One map.
  • Implemented "tower spots". These are Set locations on the map where you can build your towers by clicking on them. Towers currently have one variant and 3 upgrade levels.
  • Implemented one enemy. Named simply "enemy" has 100 hp, moves slowly, and takes 1hp from the core. No animations.
    Plays sound on core hit.
  • Implemented circuit core. This acts as your health, if an enemy reaches this then the enemey will be destroyed but you will lose 1 hp.
  • Added a music track. Credit to Meizong for his song Heartburn.

May 9th, 2021: Closed Circuit Website Launch!

After the game has been in slow development over the course of our Microsoft Software and Systems Academy, I finally have a build that is able to be hosted on WebGL.
